About Scarlatta

With 18 years of experience on stage, Scarlatta is an Argentine singer recognized for her impressive versatility. Her repertoire of over 450 songs spans from the energy of Rock and Metal, through the elegance of Jazz, to the rhythm of Pop.

Her performance format uses high-quality backing tracks, allowing for simple logistics adaptable to any space while maintaining the power and sound of a full band. Whether in intimate gatherings or massive festivals.
